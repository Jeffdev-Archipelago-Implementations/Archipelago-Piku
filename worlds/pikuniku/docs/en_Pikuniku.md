# Pikuniku

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options you need to configure
and export a config file.

## What does randomization do to this game?

Items which the player would normally acquire throughout the game have been moved around.
Hats, key items, and trophies are shuffled into the multiworld item pool, and the locations
where you would normally find them now contain checks for the multiworld.

## What is the goal of Pikuniku when randomized?

Defeat Mr. Sunshine at Sunshine HQ. To get there, you will need the Pencil Hat, the Scarecrow Face,
the Sunglasses, the Magnetic Card, the Water Hat, The Cabin Key, and A Detonator.

## What items and locations get shuffled?

Hats (Pencil Hat, Water Hat, Sunglasses, X-Ray Glasses, and cosmetic hats), key items
(Scarecrow Face, Magnetic Card, The Cabin Key, A Detonator, Apples, The Golden Tooth from the Silver Frog),
and all trophies are shuffled into the item pool.

Locations include trophies, dancing bugs, shop purchases, and story progression points such as
defeating the Giant Robots. With Coinsanity enabled, every freestanding coin in the world is also a check,
and "5 Coins" items (worth 5 coins each) are added to the pool. Shop purchases logically require
enough coins to afford them.
With Co-op Levels enabled, the Piku & Niku co-op levels and their trophies are added as checks,
gated behind a Co-op Level Access item.

## Are there any other changes made?

Not currently. The Grapple, Roll, and Kick abilities are not yet shuffled.
