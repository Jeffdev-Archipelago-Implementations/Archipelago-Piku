# Pikuniku

## What is the goal of Pikuniku when randomized?

Defeat Mr. Sunshine at Sunshine HQ. To get there, you will need the Pencil Hat, the Sunglasses, 
the Magnetic Card, the Water Hat, The Cabin Key, and A Detonator.

## What items and locations get shuffled?

Hats (Pencil Hat, Water Hat, Sunglasses, and cosmetic hats), key items
(Magnetic Card, The Cabin Key, A Detonator, Apples, The Golden Tooth from the Silver Frog),
and all trophies are shuffled into the item pool.

Locations include trophies, dancing bugs, shop purchases, and story progression points such as
defeating the Giant Robots. With Coinsanity enabled, every freestanding coin in the world is also a check,
and "5 Coins" items become progression for shop purchases.
